    Art Director for Mudfish (2021) | Summer 2021
    @ Seoul | Set Design








    Mudfish is a coming of age movie about a child who begins to see things that were previously invisible. The mudfish in the work symbolizes Sol and his family who have lost their way. At the same time, it symbolizes the innocence of Sol and her father. The work aims to make viewers reflect back at the moments when they first encountered the large and small lines and boundaries of society that we have become accustomed to, and the pieces of innocence we have lost at that time.

    1 | Responsible for set design, including props, furniture, lighting.
    2 | Created mood and reference boards and assembled pieces accordingly.
