Colorlog aims to help users perceive their emotions visually through color. A gradient is created with the user's colors, on top of which they can untangle their thoughts using words. The interface was designed with an intention of providing a space of reflection within the hecticness that is life. Facing our emotions, pleasant and unpleasant, is the first step to being able to recognize our needs, communicate them effectively, overcome challenges, and use them as a fuel to empathize with others.
